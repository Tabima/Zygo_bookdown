Chapter 8 Additional Resources:**

http://zygomycetes.org/ - descriptions and images of orders, families, and genera of zygomycetes.

http://www.nhm.ku.edu/~fungi/Monograph/Text/Mono.htm - online version of the trichomycete monograph including information about life cycles, hosts, culturing, and taxonomy.

http://keyserver.lucidcentral.org/key-server/data/0b08020c-0f0c-4908-8807-030c020a0002/media/Html/home.htm - interactive keys for trichomycete identification, including both fungal and protist species.

https://www.youtube.com/user/TrichoVideos - a series of videos made by Dr. Merlin White at Boise State University about the collection, dissection, and identification of trichomycetes.
